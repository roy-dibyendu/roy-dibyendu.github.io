Hi welcome to my personal webpage.


I am presently an Assistant Professor at Indian Institute of Information Technology Vadodara. Previously, I was a Postdoctoral-Fellow-cum-Lecturer at Indian Statistical Institute, Kolkata, India. My area of research interests are:

  1. Symmetric key cryptology

  2. Design and analysis of Boolean functions

  3. Security analysis on cryptographic protocols

  4. Quantum cryptography.


Teaching:

  1. Introduction to Cryptography and Network Security (Theory & LAB) - B.Tech and M.Tech (Winter 2021, IIIT Vadodara).

  2. Discrete Mathematics – M.Tech CrS (Winter 2020-2021, ISI Kolkata).

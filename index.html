Hi welcome to my personal webpage. I am an Assistant Professor at Indian Institute of Information Technology Vadodara. Previously, I was a Postdoctoral-Fellow-cum-Lecturer at Indian Statistical Institute, Kolkata, India. I have obtained my PhD and M.Sc. from Indian Institute of Technology Kharagpur, India. I work in the area of Cryptology.

If you are interested in working with me please drop an email.
